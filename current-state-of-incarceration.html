Current State of Incarceration in the U.S.

What is the Scale of the Correctional System the U.S.?

  • U.S. adult correctional systems supervised an estimated 6.9 million persons at yearend 2014
    • 2.2 million of these persons were incarcerated in state or federal prisons or held in local jails
    • 4.7 million of these persons were under community supervision, i.e. probation or parole
